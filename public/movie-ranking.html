The Best Movies According to Karthik

My top 3 Tamil movies of all-time.

Ratchasan

India's one of the top most psycho thriller movie. If you like thriller, this movie is highly recommended to watch.

Netru Indru Naalai

Best time travel movie in tamil. Actor Vishnu Vishal is the lead actor in both Ratchasan and this movie.

Maanaadu

Time loop concept movie. Hero Simbu got stucked in a loop and a particular day keep repeating for him. Still you dont get bored even in a single frame.
